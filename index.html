daminatila

A desktop and web music/rhythm game created as a part of my undergraduate thesis, Daminatila aims to promote the value of Gamelan Sunda to a wider audience in a new and engaging way. The game will have the player tapping the notes as if they are playing on the real gamelan as one way to do that
The early demo can be played at hakha.itch.io/daminatila

timur game shop

This is a concept of social media feed for Timur Game Shop that were done as part of my internship over there in 2024.
The design emphasizes the classiness and the coolness of their product, which mainly consist of gaming accessories, while showcasing the prominent feature and selling point of their product, like multiple color or precise button input.

fajar raggiana

An illustration made as an entry for Teh Botol Sosro's packaging design competition, in which the participant will submit an illustration to be featured in their product's packaging.
The theme for this design is the Raggiana bird from Papua, in which they are waiting for the sunrise to arrive

dragonstorm

A mockup project made for Campus Assignment. The project aims to promote Dragon Store Cafe in Bandung by creating Intellectual Property based on their target demographic while adding interesting story line with it, which involved a character that are able to “reload” back in time every time he is defeated.
